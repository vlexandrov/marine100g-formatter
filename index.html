Mean & Population Age Structure Metrics Formatter

For Marine100G Practical 2

Paste your data below and click Process to see it split into two tables - circumventing all the excel mumbo jumbo because i am not gonna do that for every 5 runs on a value change lmao

Click Copy All Values to copy all mean and age structure values, or Copy Mean Values / Copy Age Structure Values to copy each metric group separately.

Click Copy Mean labels or Copy age struct labels to copy the labels (for excel processing stuff).

NOTE: the output from copying is formatted such that each metric occupies one row. There is a new line between the two metric groups. This is following how I structured my excel sheet.
